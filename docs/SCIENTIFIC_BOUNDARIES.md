# Scientific and creative boundaries

SONIFICATION contains two deliberately separated scopes:

- the root ETQ-101 v2 profile maps declared mathematical structures to a
  symbolic MIDI note codebook and produces no PCM or rendered audio; and
- the `APP/` and `sonification/` directories are independent procedural-audio
  laboratories with their own mappings and determinism contracts.

An audio-laboratory render may reasonably be described as **audible geometry**
when its mapping, receiver, and provenance are supplied. ETQ-101 v2 itself
specifies MIDI note numbers, not canonical frequency, timbre, loudness, or
rendered sound. Its low/mid/high lane names are an authored categorical code,
not claims about physical frequency bands.

That phrase does not turn a composition into a physical measurement.

The project does not claim that:

- E8, the golden ratio, qutrit logic, an attractor, or another mapped structure
  is physically present in an audio file, MIDI file, or in nature;
- the selected-graph degree potential or its generator coefficient is uniquely
  forced by E8 or by a physical theory;
- MIDI note ordering proves a natural spectral, energetic, or perceptual
  ordering of the mathematical basis;
- a procedural render validates a physical theory;
- a deterministic classical algorithm performs a quantum computation;
- a local fingerprint proves authorship, copyright ownership, or uniqueness
  against an external catalogue;
- equal hashes imply anything beyond exact equality within the declared byte
  domain.

Future integrations with QEC data must preserve the distinction between an
input dataset, a classical model, a symbolic or sonic mapping, a MIDI receiver,
and a physical experiment. Contracts and manifests record those choices so the
layers remain inspectable.
