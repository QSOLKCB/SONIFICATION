# Contributing

Contributions are welcome when authorship and provenance remain explicit.

By submitting a contribution, you represent that you created it or have the
right to submit it, and you agree that the contribution is made available
under the [Mozilla Public License 2.0](LICENSE). You retain copyright in your
own contribution.

Please:

- describe what you changed and why;
- identify third-party material and its licence;
- preserve existing copyright, licence, provenance, and citation notices;
- add your name to [`AUTHORS.md`](AUTHORS.md) when the contribution is
  substantial; and
- do not claim authorship of work you did not create.

Modified MPL-covered files must remain available in Source Code Form under
MPL-2.0 when distributed. Larger works may use different terms for their
separate files, subject to the MPL.

Opening an issue or pull request does not transfer ownership of someone else's
work or imply endorsement by Trent Slade or QSOL-IMC.
