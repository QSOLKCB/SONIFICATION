source collaboration project.

material unless Trent Slade has requested the contribution and a separate
written contributor agreement has been signed first.

- does not grant permission to use the repository materials;
- does not transfer ownership of the submission;
- does not oblige the owner to review, retain, accept, or merge it; and
- may be closed without review when no prior written contributor agreement is

Questions about a proposed contribution may be raised through the repository's
GitHub issue interface without reproducing proprietary source or substantial
third-party material. The controlling terms are in [`LICENSE`](LICENSE).
